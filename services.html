Popular Services

Deep Cleaning

A detailed top-to-bottom cleaning designed to reset your home’s freshness and comfort.

  • Thorough scrubbing of kitchens and bathrooms
  • Dusting of baseboards, vents, and fixtures
  • Appliance exteriors and reachable interiors
  • Vacuuming and mopping across all rooms

Move-In / Move-Out

Perfect for landlords, tenants, and property owners preparing spaces for their next chapter.

  • Wipe down cabinets, drawers, and surfaces
  • Clean windowsills, doors, and light switches
  • Scrub sinks, showers, and toilets
  • Detail flooring and remove built-up grime

Office Maintenance

Reliable, routine cleanings to keep workspaces fresh, hygienic, and professional.

  • Disinfect desks, phones, and keyboards
  • Empty trash and restock common areas
  • Clean restrooms and break rooms
  • Vacuum and mop shared spaces

Bathroom Detailing

Fresh, hygienic bathrooms with a thorough top-to-bottom clean.

  • Sanitize toilets, tubs/showers, sinks, and faucet areas
  • Polish mirrors; wipe counters and splash zones
  • Wipe doors, trim, and baseboards; dust vents
  • Mop all hard flooring; remove cobwebs
  • Empty and re-line wastebaskets

Kitchen Care

A tidy, food-safe kitchen with attention to high-touch areas.

  • Wipe exteriors of fridge, oven, stove, and range hood
  • Clean microwave inside and out
  • Wipe cabinet and drawer fronts
  • Disinfect counters, backsplash, and sink
  • Sanitize knobs, handles, and light switches
  • Vacuum and mop floors; remove cobwebs
  • Empty trash and replace liner
